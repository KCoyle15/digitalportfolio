About

MTPC, Master of Technical & Professional Communication

As an individual with interests in UX Design & Research, my primary areas of study include designing multimedia products, conducting usability tests and user testing, and researching the impact of digital communications.

I am passionate about designing, prototyping, and testing products to ensure positive user experiences. Beyond this, however, I strive to create useful, human-centered designs seeing people not just as users, but humans affected by larger societal and contextual factors. Through all I do, I hope to serve as a dedicated and unwavering user advocate, advocating for those that cannot advocate for themselves.
