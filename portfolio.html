TigerPrint Usability Project

UX Research & Usability Testing

For this usability test, I served as the leader on a team of 3. We evaluated Auburn University's TigerPrint instructions and printers through a field usability test, a think-aloud methodology, post-test interview, and Microsoft's Product Reaction Cards. The report details high, medium, and low priority usability problems, demonstrating my skills in report writing.
